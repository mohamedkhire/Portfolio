Features - Scripts

Step into the exhilarating world of Squid FreeRoam PVP , a FiveM server that pushes the boundaries of immersive gameplay. Brace yourself for unmatched excitement as you engage in intense battles with our innovative match system, allowing thrilling 1v1 and 2v2 showdowns. Navigate through the arena sky ramps, conquering the ever-changing terrain and showcasing your skills in a dynamic environment.

With our unique World Menu, embark on a journey through diverse landscapes, each more captivating than the last. Prepare yourself with our Peds Aim Train script, honing your shooting skills to perfection. Experience the thrill of team-based combat as you join the green or blue team, engaging in epic battles that will leave you breathless.

Engage in custom 1v1, 2v2, 3v3, and 4v4 encounters, tailored to suit your preferences and challenges. Dive into the intense action of our Deathmatch script, where every kill counts and adrenaline runs high. Keep an eye on the leaderboard, showcasing the players with the highest kill counts, a testament to their prowess on the server.

Enhance your arsenal with our weapon attachments menu, allowing you to customize your loadout to match your playstyle. Unlock exclusive perks and privileges with our VIP menu, offering a truly elite experience.

All these awe-inspiring features and scripts are meticulously crafted by the Squid Development Team, led by the talented head developer, Mohamed Khire. Prepare yourself for an unparalleled gaming experience as you immerse yourself in the world of Squid Freeroam PVP.
